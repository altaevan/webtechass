About Us



History



Modern, sophisticated, avant-garde - this is how you can characterize the products of this brand.
The name Syder comes from Latin and translates as "both". The brand was created in 2008, and its task was to
search for innovative design solutions using innovative technologies.







Collection


The main goal of the brand is to create models with a unique design that emphasizes the
individuality of each customer. The emphasis is on the limited production of clothing and accessories for women,
making maximum demands on the quality of products and the strength of materials. Our brand serves as a starting point
in creating a unique image in the style of old money and characterizes its owner as a woman with perfect taste,
paying special attention to her image.
